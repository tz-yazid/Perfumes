Memory Team

Discover the world of perfumes with Memory Perfumes, a comprehensive online resource, magazine, and community dedicated to fragrance enthusiasts. We keep our readers in the know about the latest perfume releases, renowned fragrances, and hidden aromatic treasures. Embark on a sensory journey through time and space, guided by the captivating scents that shape our experiences. Explore their fascinating history, uncover exotic locales, and marvel at the wonders of nature with fellow perfume aficionados.


As an independent publication based in the Tunisia, Memory Perfumes is accessible in multiple languages, welcoming fragrance enthusiasts worldwide. Contribute your insights, expand your knowledge through our articles, and engage in lively discussions on our forums. We foster a respectful and inclusive environment where everyone can share their passion for fragrances.


Our diverse team of over 45 active members and contributors spans 29 countries, and Memory Perfumes is published in 18 languages, ensuring a global perspective on the enchanting world of perfumes.
